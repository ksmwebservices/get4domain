## 5. CAMPAIGN WORKFLOW

```
CLIENT REQUESTS CAMPAIGN
        ↓
Our team + AI creates content:
  Social posts, SMS, WhatsApp, Email
        ↓
AI sends approval request to vendor dashboard
Vendor gets notification: "3 posts ready for approval"
        ↓
Vendor opens dashboard → previews content
Vendor clicks [Approve] or [Request Changes]
        ↓
Vendor selects campaign channels:
  ☐ Facebook organic
  ☐ Instagram organic
  ☐ Paid ads (Meta/Google)
  ☐ WhatsApp broadcast
  ☐ SMS campaign
  ☐ Email campaign
        ↓
System shows wallet cost:
  "This campaign will use ₹85 from your wallet"
  "Current balance: ₹1,200"
  [Confirm & Start] [Cancel]
        ↓
Vendor confirms → wallet deducted
        ↓
Our team executes campaign:
  Post on their social media pages
  Send SMS via MSG91
  Send WhatsApp via their API
  Send email via their email config
  Run paid ads via their ad account
        ↓
Campaign analytics tracked in real-time
        ↓
Monthly report auto-generated
Vendor downloads PDF from dashboard
```

## 6. PAYMENT WORKFLOW

### Wallet Top-up (DomainCampaign)
```
Vendor clicks [Top-up Wallet]
Selects amount (₹999/₹2,499/₹4,999/custom)
System shows bonus credits
Razorpay opens
Vendor pays via UPI/Card/NetBanking
Payment confirmed via webhook
Wallet credited instantly
GST invoice emailed to vendor
```

### DomainApp Subscription
```
Admin creates invoice for vendor
Admin sends Razorpay payment link
Vendor opens link → pays
Webhook confirms payment
Subscription activated
GST invoice auto-generated
Email sent to vendor
Income recorded in our accounting
```
